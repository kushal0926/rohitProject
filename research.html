Research

One of the brands that has truly reshaped the landscape of natural body care is Dr. Squatch, an American personal care company founded in 2013 that has built a passionate community around nature-forward grooming products. What sets Dr. Squatch apart is its unwavering commitment to using natural, plant-derived ingredients, many of which are at least 98 % natural in origin according to international ISO 16128 standards while avoiding harsh chemicals, synthetic fragrances, and unnecessary additives that are common in these products. Their blog content covers a wide range of topics from ingredient education and grooming tips to lifestyle and natural living guides, blending practical skincare advice with personality-driven storytelling that engages customers. Dr. Squatch also stands out for its transparent ingredient disclosures and humorous, relatable voice that makes natural self-care approachable for everyday consumers. By taking inspiration from their focus on authenticity, educational content, and community engagement, we strive to build our own site into a trusted resource that not only offers wholesome body cleansing products, but also empowers our consumer to make skin-healthy choices.
